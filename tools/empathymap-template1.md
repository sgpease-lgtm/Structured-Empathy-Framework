# Empathy Map - Example Template

## Who is this map about?

- Context or situation:

## Quadrant 1: What they see
- What they observe in their environment  
- Influences shaping their understanding  
- Information or signals they pay attention to  

---

## Quadrant 2: What they hear
- Voices or sources that influence them  
- Expectations or pressures they experience  
- Guidance or narratives shaping their decisions  

---

## Quadrant 3: What they think and feel
- What matters most to them  
- Hopes, worries, or unspoken concerns  
- Assumptions or beliefs driving their behaviour  

---

## Quadrant 4: What they say and do
- How they express themselves  
- Behaviours or actions they take  
- How they show up in the situation  

---

## Pains
- Frustrations, blockers, or fears  
- Emotional or practical challenges  
- Things that make progress harder  

---

## Gains
- What success looks like for them  
- Motivators or sources of energy  
- Conditions that help them thrive  

---

## Insights and opportunities
- Patterns we notice  
- Needs that are not being met  
- Opportunities to support, align, or improve
