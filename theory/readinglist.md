# Reading Material  
## Core texts and influences

This book list brings together the foundational works that informed version 1 of the Structured Empathy Framework.  

### *Compassionate Leadership* — Manley Hopkinson  
Shows how leaders can combine high standards with deep care, reinforcing the model’s commitment to accountability with humanity.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Compassionate-Leadership-well-being-committed-high-performing/dp/0349432880/)

### *The Fearless Organization* — Amy C. Edmondson  
Defines psychological safety and provides evidence for its impact on learning, innovation, and performance.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Fearless-Organization-Psychological-Workplace-Innovation/dp/139437335X/)

### *The Compassionate Mind* — Paul Gilbert  
Provides the scientific foundation for compassion, emotional regulation, and the human threat‑soothing system — central to safe, fair interactions.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Compassionate-Mind-Compassion-Focused-Therapy/dp/1849010986/)

### *Thinking in Systems* — Donella H. Meadows  
A clear introduction to systems thinking, feedback loops, and leverage points — essential for understanding how structures shape behaviour.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/1603580557/)

### *Organizational Culture and Leadership* - Edgar H. Schein  
Explores how culture forms and operates at visible and invisible levels, providing the foundation for understanding how assumptions shape behaviour.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Organizational-Leadership-Jossey-Bass-Business-Management/dp/1119212049)

### *Just Culture* — Sidney Dekker  
Defines fair accountability by distinguishing between human error, at‑risk behaviour, and reckless behaviour.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Just-Culture-Sidney-Dekker/dp/147247578X/)

### *Dare to Lead* — Brené Brown  
Explores courage, vulnerability, and trust as leadership capabilities.
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Dare-to-Lead/dp/B07FTDHX9M/)

### *Teaming* — Amy C. Edmondson  
Explores how teams learn and collaborate in dynamic environments, reinforcing the importance of open communication and shared understanding.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Teaming-Organizations-Innovate-Compete-Knowledge/dp/B08BX5LW9M/)

### *The Fifth Discipline* — Peter M. Senge  
Defines the learning organisation and the disciplines required to sustain systemic learning.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Fifth-Discipline-practice-learning-organization/dp/1905211201/)

### *Humble Inquiry* — Edgar H. Schein & Peter A. Schein  
Explores the practice of asking questions with genuine curiosity, putting emphasis on understanding perspectives and assumptions.  
**Link:** [Available on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Humble-Inquiry-Second-Relationships-Organizations/dp/B08VCTX6K4/)
